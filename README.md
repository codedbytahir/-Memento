# MEMENTO 📖
### *Preserving the Whispers of Yesterday for the Generations of Tomorrow*

---

## ABOUT MEMENTO
**Memento** is an AI-powered biographer designed to capture the essence of a human life. It transforms fleeting memories and scattered stories into a beautifully structured, polished biography. By combining the power of voice interaction with advanced generative AI, Memento ensures that the wisdom, laughter, and legacy of our loved ones are never lost to time.

---

## MEMENTO STORY
In a world that moves too fast, we often find ourselves wishing we had asked one more question, recorded one more laugh, or preserved one more story from our elders. The "Memento Story" began with a simple realization: **memories are the only true inheritance we leave behind.** 

We created Memento for a hackathon to bridge the gap between generations. It was born from the desire to give every person—regardless of their technical skill—the ability to sit down with a "digital biographer" who listens with empathy and crafts their life's journey into a narrative that can be held, read, and cherished forever. 

*Memento isn't just an app; it’s a bridge across time.*

---

## TECH STACK
Memento is built with a robust, modern stack to ensure a seamless and soulful user experience:

*   **Frontend**: [Next.js 14](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router) for a lightning-fast, SEO-friendly interface.
*   **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) with [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) for fluid, emotional animations.
*   **State Management**: [Zustand](https://github.com/pmndrs/zustand) for lightweight and reactive data handling.
*   **Cloud Infrastructure**: [Amazon Web Services (AWS)](https://aws.amazon.com/)
    *   **AWS Bedrock**: Orchestrating the AI intelligence.
    *   **AWS DynamoDB**: Scalable NoSQL database for session and transcript storage.
    *   **AWS S3**: Secure storage for the photos that anchor your memories.
    *   **AWS SES**: Delivering the finished legacy directly to your inbox.
*   **Document Generation**: [jsPDF](https://github.com/parallax/jsPDF) and [html2canvas](https://html2canvas.hertzen.com/) to create beautiful, printable PDF biographies.

---

## AI MODEL USED
Memento is powered by **Amazon Nova Lite** via **AWS Bedrock**. 

We chose **Nova Lite** for its incredible ability to understand nuance, maintain the speaker's original voice, and transform raw, conversational transcripts into professional, structured prose. It acts as the "biographer's soul," removing filler words while keeping the heartbeat of the story intact.

## FEATURES
*   **📸 Visual Anchors**: Upload up to 5 cherished photos to trigger memories and ground your story.
*   **🎙️ Voice-First Interview**: A natural, conversational interface that allows users to *speak* their life story rather than typing it.
*   **🧠 Intelligent Synthesis**: AI that doesn't just transcribe—it edits, organizes chronologically, and polishes your words into a literary format.
*   **📄 PDF Legacy**: Instant generation of a beautiful PDF biography, ready to be shared with family or printed as a keepsake.
*   **📧 Direct Delivery**: Automatically sends the finished story to your email, ensuring your legacy is safely stored.
*   **✨ Immersive UI**: A "Cream & Navy" aesthetic designed to feel like a high-end stationery shop, making the process of storytelling feel sacred.

---

## WHY IT IS BENEFICIAL
*   **Generational Connection**: It gives grandchildren a way to "hear" the stories of their grandparents in a structured, readable format.
*   **Dignity in Storytelling**: It empowers the elderly to reflect on their lives and see their experiences treated with the professional polish they deserve.
*   **Combatting Memory Loss**: For those in the early stages of cognitive decline, Memento serves as a vital tool to lock in stories before they fade.
*   **Simplicity**: No complex forms or technical barriers. If you can talk, you can write a book.
